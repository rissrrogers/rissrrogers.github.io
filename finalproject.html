What's your sign?

Have you ever wondered what your birthday says about you? Our app has the solution for you, by clicking on the range of dates you can not only find out your sign, but you can read a bit about what the stars have to say about you. If you decide to click next you can also receive information about your horoscope for the month of May. If not you can click on the back button and look up a friend or family members sign. If you click on the bitmoji it will take you to the end of the app, where there is a slight disclaimer and a thank you for participating.
